SECTION 5: DUES, PAYMENTS AND FUNDING

1. Effective 9/1/86: Yearly dues are waived per year. The troop conducts a few money-raising projects that cover dues and most fewws for activities..

2. IMPORTANT: If parents feel that the cost of a Troop activity is beyond the family's ability to pay, they should call the Scoutmaster, who will make confidential arrangements for funding or for a loan, as appropriate. NO SCOUT SHALL MISS AN ACTIVITY FOR FINANCIAL REASONS.
