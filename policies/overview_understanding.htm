Overview & Understanding

The attached GUIDEBOOK outlines the basis upon which TROOP 503 has built its reputation as a fun, safety conscious, and educational organization which is available to all boys of scouting age living in the Winchester area. You should keep the GUIDEBOOK for future reference as you will find it contains lots of useful information.
